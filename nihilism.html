Philosophy:
Nietzsche & Shopenhauer

This is the project which I am currently working on. I want to shoot images that reflect ideas of philosiphers to try and truly capture an images that speak 1000 words. The photo project will take form in a diptych/triptych(2/3 panel photo series) and will be themed around philosophy.

Heavily influenced Arthur Shopenhauer and Friedrich Nietzsche, I aim to visually represent the dark, negative view of the world. Across the series of images, the vibes will shift from dark and pessimistic to bright and optimistic to illustrate Nietzsche's idea of using all the suffering around an individual and channeling it into strength to overcome tragedy on the path to becoming an "Übermensch". By becoming the "Übermensch"(roughly translates to Superman) one must overcome their demons to become the most formidible version of themselves.

By the conclusion of this project, I hope I will have been able to convey these ideas through film photography :)
